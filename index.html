Crystal Collectors!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal your will add a specific amount of points to you total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the ramdom number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Every time the game restarts, the values of each crystal changes.
